Mandal Members

The Karyakari Mandal, trustees and volunteer teams who bring Fortcha Raja to life every year.

Karyakari Mandal Volunteer Teams Founders & Trustees

Fortcha Raja is a volunteer-run sarvajanik (public) Ganpati mandal. For generations, the residents, traders and well-wishers of Fort have come together — as a managing committee and as hundreds of volunteers — to organise the utsav with faith, discipline and seva.

Below is the structure of our managing committee and teams. Names and photos are being added — the mandal will publish the current office bearers here.

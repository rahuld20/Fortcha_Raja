Privacy & Refund Policy

How we handle your information and payments.

Privacy

  • We collect only what you submit via forms (name, contact, seva/navas details) to fulfil your request.
  • We do not sell or share your data. We never store card data on this website.
  • Analytics, if enabled, is used only to improve the site.

Payments & security

All payments are processed by a PCI-compliant gateway (Razorpay/PayU). No card details touch this site.

Refund policy

  • Donations are voluntary and generally non-refundable; 80G receipts are issued for eligible donations.
  • Seva / prasad / passes: state your refund/cancellation window and process here.
  • For any payment issue, contact us with your transaction reference.
